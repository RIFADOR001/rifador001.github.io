What is Ricochet Robots?

EDIT Multiclip is a program to keep information handy.

EDIT The "Notes" application in iOS is very helpful to keep important information close to you. But it is not always easy to keep your notes organized. Multiclip allows you to write notes or paste links and tag them with a keyword. Later on you can find your information simply writing the keyword that you chose.

About Multiclip

The app is intuitive to use. You can add or remove data, check your list, write notes in your entries to get a better reference and more context. You can even move data directly from and to your clipboard!!! Better than just a clipboard is a multiclip.

Convenient organization

Instead of looking for your notes in a huge file or folder, you have everything you need to remember in only one place. But things are easy to find using the keyword.

Keyword suggestions

You don't need to remember the keywords that you used. When you start typing you will find suggestions that match your text. It makes it easy to find the key that you need!!!
